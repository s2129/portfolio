１.Explanation of Manga Ranking

This ranking page presents information on interesting manga in a ranking format. Regarding how the rankings are determined, it is not based on popular vote or consensus among readers; instead, I personally select three manga titles that I find enjoyable and present them. Therefore, if a particular manga is not ranked, it's because I haven't deemed it as such. Please keep in mind that these rankings are based solely on personal opinions.

The story revolves around Yoichi Isagi, a second-year high school student, aiming to become the world is greatest striker. In his quest, he confronts numerous formidable opponents in soccer matches.

２.3rd Tsubasa-kun Wa Akanuketainoni

Writer　Oto Obana

The protagonist of this series is Tsubasa, who decides to live in a share house upon entering high school with the goal of becoming stylish. However, he finds himself at the mercy of the eccentric individuals living there. Despite being thrown off by them, Tsubasa continues to pursue his goal of becoming fashionable. The manga depicts Tsubasa is daily life filled with chaos as he strives for style amidst the unpredictable residents of the share house.
