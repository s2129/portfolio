BLUELOCK

BLUE LOCK is a manga series by creators Muneyuki Kaneshiro and Yuusuke Nomura, featuring the protagonist Yoichi Isagi, a second-year student at Ichinan High School. Isagi is a forward in Ichinan's soccer team. In the final moments of the Saitama Prefectural High School Soccer Championship, the ball comes to Isagi, but instead of taking the shot himself, he prioritizes passing to a teammate, resulting in their team's defeat. He deeply regrets not taking the shot and missing the chance to advance to the national tournament. However, at that moment, he is chosen as one of the enhanced players for the BLUE LOCK project and is inspired by the words of the project's director, Jinpachi Ego, to change his fate. With the condition that failure in the BLUE LOCK project means exclusion from the Japanese national team, Isagi resolves to become the world's greatest striker.
