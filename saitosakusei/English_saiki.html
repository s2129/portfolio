The Disastrous Life of Saiki K

Saiki Kusuo no Psi-nan is a comedy manga depicting the daily life of Saiki Kusuo, a high school student with psychic abilities. Saiki's goal is to live a life without standing out, but he constantly finds himself entangled with amusing characters who disrupt this goal. Saiki's psychic powers are potent yet often uncontrollable, causing him considerable trouble. Many of Saiki's classmates are the source of his problems due to their troublesome personalities. Despite being depicted as a second-year high school student, Saiki is not repeating his year in the Sazae-san style; rather, he has the ability to turn back time due to a certain calamity.
