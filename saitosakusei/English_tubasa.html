Tsubasa-kun Wa Akanuketainoni

This series follows the protagonist Tsubasa, who moves to Tokyo upon entering high school. Tsubasa has a strong aversion to anything fashionable or trendy, yet harbors a desire to become stylish himself. Therefore, he decides to move to Tokyo and live in a share house. His roommates in the share house are all strong personalities and are far from the stylish image Tsubasa aspires to. The story depicts the chaotic and comedic daily life as Tsubasa interacts with his eccentric roommates, classmates, and others, all while aiming to pursue his own sense of fashion.
